Sugar Cookies

Recipe

Ingredients List

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar (plus extra for rolling)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp almond extract
  • 2-3 tbsp sprinkles or coloured sugar
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 350oF. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar using an electric mixer or stad mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy.
  4. beat in the egg, vanilla extract, and almond extract, mixing until well combined.
  5. Graduallya ss the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing just until the doug comes together.
  6. If the dough is too soft, cover it with plastic wrap and chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes to 1 hour. thsi helps make rolling easier and gives the cookies a better texture.
  7. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough and roll the into balls. Roll each ball in granulated sugar to coat, place the dough balls about 2 inches apart on the prepared baking sheets.
  8. Gently press each cookie ball with the bottom of a glass or your hand to slightly flatten them. You can sprinkle a little extra sugar on top if you like.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are just beginning to turn golden. the centers may still look a little soft, but they will firm up as they cool.
  10. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely
