Biscuits


"Whey biscuits" by grongar is licensed under CC BY 2.0 .

Recipe

Ingredients list

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 3/4 cup milk

    • Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 450oF. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.
    2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar until well combined.
    3. Add the cold butter cubes to the dry ingredients. Using a pastry cutter, fork, or your fingers, work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs with pea-sized pieces of butter throughout.
    4. Pour in the milk and stir with a spoon or spatula until just combined. Be careful not to overwork the dough; it should be slightly sticky.
    5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface. Gently fold the dough over itself 3-4 times to create layers, then pat it into a rectangle about 1-inch thick.
    6. Use a biscuit cutter (or a glass) to cut out biscuits. Press straight down, don't twist, as twisting can prevent the biscuits from rising properly. Place the biscuits on the prepared baking sheet.
    7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the biscuits are golden brown on top.
