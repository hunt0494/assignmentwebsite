Coffee Cake


Evan-Amos, CC0, via Wikimedia Commons

Recipe

Ingredients List

For The Cake

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream
For the Streusel Topping
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 350oF
  2. Grease and flour a 9-inch round or square cake pan, or line with parchment paper.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat the softened butter and granulated sugar together until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Alternately add the dry flour mixture and sour cream to the butter mixture, beggining and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.
  6. In a medium bowl, combine the brown sugar, flour, and cinnamon. Add the softened butter and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in the chopped nuts.
  7. Pour half of the cake batter into the prepared pan and spread it evenly. Sprinkle half of the streusel mixture over the batter. Add the remaining batter and smooth it out. Sprinkle the remaining mixture over the top.
  8. Bake for 45-55 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean or with a few moist crumbs.
    9. let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, the transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
